Abstract

Ontology for representing European high-risk medical devices, manufacturers, regulatory risk classes, identified risks, incidents and field safety corrective actions based on EUDAMED-related data

Introduction back to ToC

This ontology models a catalogue of European high-risk medical devices and in vitro diagnostic devices.
